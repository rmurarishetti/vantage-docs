## Why a CLA?

Vantage Wealth is licensed under AGPL-3.0. The CLA gives the project maintainer flexibility to:
- Offer commercial or subscription-based versions in the future
- Dual-license the project without re-obtaining consent from all contributors
- Maintain a clean IP chain

This is the same model used by Redis, Elastic, and HashiCorp before their commercial pivots.

## How to sign

Add this comment to your first pull request:

```
I have read the CLA and agree to its terms.
```

PRs from contributors who haven't signed will not be merged.
