## Modes

### Equal split
The simplest mode. Enter the total, choose how many people, and Vantage divides it equally. Handles rounding automatically.

### Itemized split
Per-item assignment with shared costs:
1. Add items with prices
2. Assign each item to one or more people
3. Add shared tax % and service charge %
4. Vantage computes each person's exact share

### Smart split (OCR)
Point your camera at a receipt and Vantage:
1. Uses Google ML Kit (on-device OCR) to extract the text
2. Sends it to Claude Haiku to parse items, prices, tax, and service charge
3. Pre-fills the itemized split form automatically

Works with most printed restaurant receipts.

---

## Groups

Create groups for recurring bill splits (e.g. housemates, regular dinner friends). Groups maintain running tallies of who owes whom, with a **simplify debts** algorithm that minimises the number of transactions needed to settle.

Example: 3 people, multiple shared bills → Vantage tells you the minimum payment graph (A pays B $12, B pays C $8) instead of tracking each bill separately.

---

## Settlement

Mark debts as settled via:
- **PayNow** (Singapore) — show QR code
- **UPI** (India) — show UPI ID
- **Cash** — manual confirmation
- **Bank transfer** — record reference

Settled debts are archived with a history log.
